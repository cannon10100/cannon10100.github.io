Research Interests

Generally speaking, I am interested in robotic autonomy. This field takes many forms, from classical control to modern machine learning. At the end of the day, I am fascinated by complex problems that are dangerous or tedious for humans to solve. My research incorporates aspects of control theory, statistics, optimization, formal mathematics, and good old search and planning.
