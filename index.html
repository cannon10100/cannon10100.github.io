Research Interests

Generally speaking, I am interested in robotic autonomy. This field takes many forms and admits many approaches, from classical control to modern machine learning. At the end of the day, I am fascinated by complex problems that are dangerous or tedious for humans to solve alone. My research incorporates aspects of control theory, statistics, optimization, formal mathematics, and good old computer science. Get in touch if you'd like to know more!
